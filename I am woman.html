I Am Woman

“I Am Woman” is a typographic poster project inspired by Helen Reddy’s 1971 anthem. Created during the second wave of feminism, the song became a rallying cry for women asserting their strength, resilience, and voice. The designs aim to visually echo the power and pride in the lyrics, using bold layout, expressive type, and a sense of rhythmic movement that mirrors both protest and empowerment. The project stands as a call for continued action, reminding viewers that the fight for equality is ongoing — and so is the roar.
